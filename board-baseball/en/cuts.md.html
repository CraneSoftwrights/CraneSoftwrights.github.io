In this cuts/ directory are directories of utility SVG and PDF files used in the preparation of materials for later burning.

There are no baseball-themed SVG or PDF files in thee directories.

The dimensions using low numbers 24 and under are in inches, height x width.

The dimensions using high numbers 200 and up are in millimetres, height x width.

The raw materials for these SVG files are found in the design/design-board-baseball-crane.svg file.
